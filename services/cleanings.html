Cleanings

What to Expect

For most people, a cleaning consists of polishing your teeth (children), or scaling and polishing for adults. I do not use ultrasonic scalers as I personally find them uncomfortable and noisy. I think I can remove tartar buildup more comfortably by hand. I also do not routinely do fluoride treatments. If indicated, I prefer you to use fluoride at home on a regular, even daily basis. This will be far more effective and beneficial to you than an in-office fluoride treatment done once or twice a year.

Cleanings also include flossing your teeth, as well as discussing areas that need improving. Patients with more significant issues may come in more frequently for cleanings as needed.
